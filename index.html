Potential Project

I've been trying to write a book for a while now. I want to use the narrative device to explore questions of how what it means to be human is understood through a modern, computationally-informed context. A lot of the story is centered around the pursuit of understanding through technology and a lack of tangible, social interaction (and either the futility or ability to do so). I feel being able to create an interactive, online presence for this story is apt for the themes, and can lend otherwise inaccessible accompanyments to the story (i.e music, non-linear progression, interactive footnotes, etc)
